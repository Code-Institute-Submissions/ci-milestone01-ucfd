About Paul

As a London-based performer with over 20 years’ experience in the music industry, Paul Middleton has performed around the world, including the USA, Jordan, Spain, Germany, Belgium, Ireland, and all around the UK. He is a talented international singer and songwriter, currently with two successful albums already released, and a third album scheduled to be released in October 2018. In addition to both albums, (Covers and Covers 2) he has also written the title track for the 2017 motion picture “Hanging in There”.
