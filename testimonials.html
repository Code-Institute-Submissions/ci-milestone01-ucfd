Testimonials & Clients

Paul works closely with several cancer charities, and profits from both Covers and Covers 2 go to Cancer Research. On social media, he has developed quite the following from around the globe, and has been personally invited to represent numerous charities.


Paul’s cover of “Hands to Heaven” was nominated for Most Moving Video in the 2014 RightOutTV Music & Video Awards, and his cover of “Can’t Stop the Feeling” was a huge online success.
