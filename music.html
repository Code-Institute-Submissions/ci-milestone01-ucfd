Paul's Music

listen | buy | share

To showcase his voice and talent, Paul is well-known and recognised for singing a broad range of popular songs that are suited to any occasion. From huge classic 80s hits by Wham! and A-Ha, to modern world music like Bruno Mars, Justin Timberlake, and Daft Punk, or to gentle songs of Adele and Cyndi Lauper. Paul loves it all!
